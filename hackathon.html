Topics for Hackathon

Build apps to enhance education systems with features like resume-building tools, personalized learning, and better resource accessibility.

Develop tech-driven solutions addressing critical health issues like mental health, remote care, or chronic disease management.

Create solutions to improve accessibility, inclusion, and quality of life for people with disabilities.
